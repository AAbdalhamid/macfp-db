#### Notes

|----|------------|------------|
|R4  | 0.008333   | 0.03906    |
|R5  | 0.004167   | 0.01953    |
|R6  | 0.002778   | 0.01302    |
|R7  | 0.002083   | 0.00977    |
|R8  | 0.001667   | 0.00781    |
